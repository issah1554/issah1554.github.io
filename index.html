Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Portal

How long does it take to get started?

Create your first project in under 5 minutes. Our intuitive interface makes setup quick and easy, so you can start managing your projects immediately.

Who are the collaborators?

Our collaborators are skilled professionals vetted by our quality team. We have experts in design, development, content creation, marketing, and more ready to work on your projects.

What types of projects work best?

Portal is perfect for creative work, development, design, content creation, marketing campaigns, research projects, and any collaborative effort that benefits from our structured workflow.

How does the quality control process work?

Every deliverable goes through a two-stage review process. First, our quality control team reviews for technical accuracy and completeness. Then, your project manager does a final review to ensure all requirements are met.
